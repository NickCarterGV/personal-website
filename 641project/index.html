CIS 641 Semester Project Information

For our semester project, my partner (Cam Schneider) and I are creating a web-based rougelike baseball videogame. A couple features of our game include the following:

  • Simple baseball diamond interface with stats and player names listed
  • Stat-based, chance gameplay. Players can level up several different stats to increase the odds of specific game functions happening
  • Authentication and save states to save your progress and pick up where you left off
  • Items that can be gained and used to increase statistical odds for certains gameplay functions

This project will reflect similar gameplay to that of Balatro or Slay the Spire, where the player will complete different "runs," level up their stats, and use items to try and win their current run. After each run, players will have gained information on strategies and stat increases that make their chances for the next run even better. This encapsulates the idea of a rougelike, but with a twist for all baseball enthusiasts.
